About This Project

Future Forecast is a data-driven project that uses polynomial regression on historical datasets to predict the trajectory of global challenges and technological developments from 2000 to 2100.

For established trends like climate change and global health metrics, we use data from reputable sources and apply mathematical models to extend these trends into the future. For more speculative scenarios, we've created custom algorithmic predictions based on current technological trajectories and expert opinions.

Our goal is to visualize potential futures and encourage investment in sustainable solutions that can address the world's most pressing challenges.

Methodology

For UN Development Goals and environmental challenges, we used polynomial regression on historical data series to project future trends. The model uses the following approach:

  • Data collection from reliable sources (UN, WHO, World Bank)
  • Data cleaning and normalization
  • Polynomial regression (degree 1-3 depending on metric)
  • Projection with confidence intervals

For future technologies and wildcard scenarios, we created algorithmic models that incorporate:

  • Current R&D investment trajectories
  • Historical precedents for similar technological developments
  • Expert opinions on timeline feasibility

Note: These predictions are speculative and should be viewed as potential scenarios rather than definitive forecasts.
